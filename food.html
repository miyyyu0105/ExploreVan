Trees Cheesecake + Organic Coffee

I love to go to Trees coffee to treat myself to a good cup of coffee and rich and dense cheesecake.
It's a great place to take your laptop to and work as there are few sockets around that you can use.
One thing though, you might think their cheesecake is a bit pricy ($8) but it's totally worth it!

Cheesecake Etc

Solid classic Cheesecake if you like the rich style.
It's a light and strong creamy piece so it'll be the best to combine it with a nice cup of coffee or tea. You definitely want to try their New York Cheesecake with strawberry sauce. It blows your mind!
They open from 7pm so when you're craving for a late night snack, this is the place to go.
You'll also enjoy the dark atmosphere and live music.

Uncle Tetsu's Japanese Cheesecake

It's Japanese style cheesecake which is light and fluffy and soft texture.
It is not heavy like the New York style cheese cakes. You can eat the entire cake by yourself.
Personally I think the original is the best but they also have a matcha cheesecake.

Castella Cheesecake

The cheese tarts are the BEST.
When it's fresh, it's even better! I recommend you going there as soon as they open (12pm) or
just before they close(11pm) so that you can eat it fresh.

Billy Button Dessert Bar

Desserts are extra tasty when they are photogenic and cute! And this is the place to check out!
All of the desserts are beautifully presented in their dishes. And they taste really good too.
The place is pretty small and it gets busy later at night so if you don't want to wait in line,
it would be smart to go early.

Thierry

Forget bars or clubs, Thierry Chocolaterie Patisserie Cafe is THE place in Vancouver to hang out.
It is a French dessert hotspot that opens for the breakfast crowd at 7 a.m. and
closes with the drunk and stumbling at midnight every day of the week.
Cakes, macarons and drinks... everything there is wonderful.

Luckey's Doughnuts by 49th Parallel Coffee

One of my favourite spots for donuts in Vancouver.
They excel at creative flavour combinations, and do an exceptional job with the classics as well.
Parallel 49 coffee is always a treat.

Cartems Donuts

Here's my another favorite donut place! They have a wide selection, and update it seasonally.
They also have vegan and gluten free donuts.
Their prices are fair and their customer service is fantastic.

Earnest Ice Cream

It's SERIOUSLY GOOD!
The quality of the ice cream is always so consistent and the flavours are always delicious and creative. My favorite of all time is Whiskey Hazelnut.
My friends highly recommended Matcha and London Fog flavor.
It would be perfect to have these ice cream in summer.

Kokoro Tokyo Mazesoba

This Mazesoba (no soup) is actually really good.
I haven't tried the actual ramen (with soup) yet but the reviews said
"It was amazing... the flavours were very different in comparison to your typical bowl of ramen with with a much creamier broth base". So try them out!

The Old Spaghetti Factory

For a very reasonable price, you get what's essentially a 3-course home-cooked meal, with an additional coffee or tea at the end.
The quality is quite good, though it isn't exactly outstanding.
Still, this is one of the best places in town if you're dining on a budget.

Steamworks Brewing Company

If you like drinking beer, this is the place to go.
You can get a good sampling of a variety of flavors.
